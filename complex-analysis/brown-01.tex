\section{Basic Algebraic Properties}

\subsection{Exercises}

\textbf{2.2}

Some interesting properties

$$
z + \bar{z} = (a+ib) + (a-ib) = 2a = 2\Re (z)
$$

Similarly,
$$
z - \bar{z} = (a+ib) - (a-ib) = 2ib = 2\Im (z)
$$

Following the same mechanics,
$$
\Re (iz) = \Re (i(a+ib)) = \Re (ai-b) = -\Im (z)
$$
And
$$
\Im (iz) = \Im (ai -b) = \Re (z)
$$
